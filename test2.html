About the Symposium

Purpose

While modern algorithms are on the rise in clinical research and healthcare industry, personnel and trainees in hospitals have little chance to keep up with the current state of the art in artificial intelligence (AI). However, the key to successfull implementation of medical AI also relies on expertise of clinical professionals. Therefore we want to give you a head start in the medical system of tomorrow.

We want you!

Are you interested in novel approaches to classical medical problems? Are you curious about what´s possible with machine learning algorithms in the present medical system, and want to hear from the experts where they want to take it in the future?

Visit our symposium and meet the leading figures of the field!

FAQ

What is LMU AIM?

LMU AIM is a student-led initiative at LMU Munich teaching students and healthcare professionals on AI and its applications in medicine.

Will there be an on-site event? How can i join and are there any conditions?

The symposium is planned as a hybrid event (online and on-site). Depending on official COVID regulations we might have to switch to a solely online event. For the on-site event we will adhere closely to the official hygiene concept. Currently (September 21st 2021) you have to follow the german "3G"-rule to join on-site. You need a valid certificate of vaccination, certificate of recovery from COVID-19 or PCR-test (not older than 72h). Because of limited on-site visitor capacity and COVID-19 regulations you will have to confirm your on-site participation even if you registered with on-site preference. Links to an online confirmation form will be sent two weeks before the symposium.
