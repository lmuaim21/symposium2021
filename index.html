About the symposium

Purpose

While modern algorithms are on the rise in clinical research and healthcare industry, personnel and trainees in hospitals have little chanceto keep up with the current state of the art in artificial intelligence. However, the key to successfull implementation of medical AI also relies on expertise of clinical professionals. Therefore we want to give you a head start in the medical system of tomorrow.

We want you!

Are you interested in novel approaches to classical medical problems? Are you curious about what´s possible with machine learning algorithms in the present medical system, and want to hear from the experts where they want to take it in the future?
We aim to educate medical students and physicians internationally and are looking forward to your registration!

FAQ

What is LMU AIM?

LMU AIM is a student-led initiative that supports education and projects in artificial intelligence in medicine
at the Faculty of Medicine at LMU Munich.

Will there be an On-site event?

The symposium is planned as a hybrid event (online and face-to-face). Due to federal regulations imposed as a result of the SARS-CoV 2 pandemic we might have to switch to solely online events. For On-site events we follow the location hosts hygiene concept.

I registered but there was no confirmation e-mail. What should I do?

Confirmation e-mails with further information on the symposium (e.g. program book, webinar links) will be sent in groups. Therefore it may take up to 3 days until the registration is confirmed. Please also check your spam folder. If you haven´t received a confirmation E-mail after 3 days you may contact us via the contact button below.
